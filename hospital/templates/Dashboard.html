Kamala Nehru Hospital is a 450 bedded Multi Specialty General Hospital run by Pune Municipal Corporation. It is one of the big hospital, run by Pune Municipal Corporation. It is situated in the busy area of Mangalwar Peth, about ½ kilometer from the famous historical monument Shaniwar Wada and accessible through various local transport bus routes. It was taken over by Pune Municipal Corporation on 1st Sept.1966 from the Church of Scotland Mission. The total area housing the complex is 1.7 acres (i.e.74,400 sq.ft.) with a built up area of 2,29,595 sq.ft. with an adequate area for parking. All the departments are well connected through a computerized EPABX system, Residential quarters for resident medical officers, staff nurses, Houseman including Superintendent’s residence are located in the same complex. Kamala Nehru Hospital is recognized as a teaching hospital for both Undergraduate and Postgraduate training, since 1990.
